Balloon Care

Keeping Your Balloons Looking Beautiful

  • ✓Keep balloons away from sharp objects and rough surfaces.
  • ✓Avoid extreme heat, direct sunlight and cold — these affect float time.
  • ✓Foil balloons can last 3–5 days or longer if kept in a warm room.
  • ✓If transporting, keep balloons inside the car and tie ribbons securely.
  • ✓Latex balloons are treated for extended float time — handle with care.
  • ✓Questions? WhatsApp us — we're always happy to advise.
