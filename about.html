How It Began

A Passion for
Beautiful Moments

MyBalloons was born out of a simple belief — that gifts should feel as special as the person receiving them. What started as a love for creating beautiful arrangements for friends and family quickly grew into something far bigger.

Today we're one of Dublin's most trusted balloon gift studios, creating bespoke arrangements for hundreds of customers every year — from intimate birthday surprises to large-scale event installations.
