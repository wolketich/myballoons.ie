Case Study

Personalised Birthday Balloon Gift

A custom birthday arrangement with name foil balloons, coordinated latex clusters and a luxury ribbon finish — delivered to a home in south Dublin.

The Brief

A customer in Dublin 6 reached out looking for a birthday gift for her friend turning 30. She wanted something more impressive than a standard bunch of flowers but also more personal than a generic gift. She had seen a photo of our personalised arrangements on Instagram and wanted something similar — gold and blush pink tones, her friend's name in foil letters, and a generous, full arrangement that would make a real impression when delivered to the door.

What We Created

We created a full birthday arrangement built around oversized gold foil letter balloons spelling out 'SARAH' as the centrepiece, flanked by blush pink and champagne latex balloon clusters with satin ribbon tails. We added a matching '30' foil number balloon at the front and finished the whole arrangement with a personalised kraft gift tag. The colour palette was warm and feminine without being garish — exactly what the customer had asked for. The arrangement was assembled fresh on the morning of delivery and delivered in a protective sleeve to ensure it arrived in perfect condition.

The Result

The arrangement was delivered to Sarah's home at 10am — two hours before she was due home from brunch with her family. The customer's partner had arranged for someone to be home to receive it, and photographed the arrangement set up in the hallway. The customer shared the photos with us on Instagram, along with a message saying her friend was 'absolutely speechless' when she walked in. The photos were liked over 200 times. She placed a follow-up order three months later for a Valentine's Day gift.

"I couldn't believe how beautiful it looked in person. Sarah literally cried when she walked in. Exactly what I asked for, and delivered on time without any hassle. I'll never go anywhere else."

— Emma, Dublin 6
